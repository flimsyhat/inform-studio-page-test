The hard part is done—its time to share your work! A custom illustration is a great way to promote your research, and can be used for conference presentations, posters, lab websites, social media, and much more.

How it works:

1

You send us a copy of the paper and any ideas* you have for illustration.

*Written description, photos, hastily scribbled napkin sketches

2

We produce a number of sketches from your description, ideas for where to start.

3

We work with you to refine the illustration, going through multiple drafts.

4

When you're satisfied, we produce a high-resolution render, ready for publication.
