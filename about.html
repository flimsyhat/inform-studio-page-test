About

Hi! I'm Sean Kelley.

I started Inform Studio to help scientists and science communicators share their work more effectively. I believe that beautiful and accurate depictions of scientific research are one of the best ways to inform and inspire others.

I originally studied to be a physicist, but while in school I fell in love with 3D modeling and animation. Now, science illustration combines both worlds!

Aside from my work, I enjoy surfing, learning new musical instruments, and traveling (currently in Buenos Aires, Argentina).
